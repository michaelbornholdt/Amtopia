About Me

My work involves navigating the complexities of bureaucratic processes, fostering cultural change, and implementing the newest technologies to the Behörden, Ministerien and Ämter of this country.

Like many, I strongly believe that fundamentally remaking the processes of our Verwaltung can revive the trust in the German government and society. But achieving this will take more grit and talent than we currently have in this field!

With my technical background and focus on AI product development, I bring a unique perspective from the "Maschinenraum" on the challenges and opportunities of digital transformation.
